About Us

Bizeco, the Business and economics Club of DPS Rohini, is dedicated to nurturing future leaders and entrepreneurs. We provide a platform for students to explore business strategies, enhance financial literacy, and develop essential leadership skills. Through interactive sessions, workshops, and competitions, we inspire creativity, critical thinking, and a passion for economic excellence. Join us to embark on your journey towards a successful future in business and economics.
